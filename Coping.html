Stress Management Tips

Take regular breaks and practice deep breathing.

Take slow deep breaths for 5 minutes.

Take a 10 minute break every hour.

Reach out to a friend or counsellor.

Common Challenges Faced by NWU Students

  • Academic pressure and deadlines
  • Adjusting to university life
  • Financial stress
  • Balancing social and academic responsibilities

Healthy Daily Habits

Building small, consistent habits can improve your mental wellbeing over time. Try to maintain a routine that includes rest, study and personal time.

  • Exercise regularly even short walks
  • Eat balanced meals
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • Avoid last minute cramming
