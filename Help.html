When Should You Seek Help?

It is important to seek help if you feel overwhelmed, anxious or unable to cope with daily responsibilities. Early support can prevent challenges from becoming more serious.

  • Feeling constantly stressed or anxious
  • Difficulty concentrating on studies
  • Loss of motivation
  • Feeling isolated or unsupported
